Ugly Dip

Description

I went to a family event, and one of my cousins made this ugly dip recipe.

The women couldn't get enough of it.

It was gone in 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, drained
  • 1 (7 ounce) can Mexican-style corn, drained
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. tir cream cheese, mayonnaise, diced tomatoes, corn, and green onion together in a bowl.
  2. Serve immediately or chill.
