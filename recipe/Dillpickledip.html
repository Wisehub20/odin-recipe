Dill Pickle Dip

Description

This was served at our Easter lunch and was devoured.

Serve with chips or crackers.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 cup chopped dill pickles, or more to taste
  • ¼ cup finely chopped sweet onion
  • 2 tablespoons pickle juice, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Stir cream cheese in a bowl with a wooden spoon until smooth.
  2. Stir in dill pickles, onion, pickle juice, dill weed, salt,
  3. and pepper until evenly distributed.
  4. Refrigerate before serving, at least 1 hour.
